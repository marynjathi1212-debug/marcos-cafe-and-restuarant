Our Story

Born from a
love of good food

Marco's started as a small corner café in 2018 — just a wooden counter, an old espresso machine, and a chef with too many recipes and not enough excuses to cook them.

Today we're a full restaurant, but the spirit hasn't changed. Every morning starts the same way: fresh ingredients, slow prep, and the smell of something good already in the oven.

We cook the kind of food we want to eat ourselves — honest, generous, and made with actual care. Come as you are. Stay as long as you like.

— Marco
